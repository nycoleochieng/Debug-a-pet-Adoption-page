all about kentucky

Admitted as the 15th state in 1792, the Commonwealth of Kentucky is a landlocked southeastern state known as the "Bluegrass State," famous for horse racing (Kentucky Derby), bourbon distilleries, and bluegrass music. Its diverse geography includes the world's longest cave system, Mammoth Cave National Park, and major rivers like the Ohio and Mississippi.
